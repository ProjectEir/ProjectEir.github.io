Background

We created this project because clinical trails are the future of medicine. We were also chocked how bad and non interactive the current tool is considering how many that are using it.

This project is very close to heart for us. Everyone know someone who have died or is suffering from a disease. For example in US 1 in 2 woman and 1 in 3 men will develop cancer in their lifetime, and that is only one disease out of thouseands.
