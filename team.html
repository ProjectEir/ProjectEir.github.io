Team Snortra

Background

We created this project because clinical trails are the future of medicine. We were also shocked how bad and non interactive the current tool is considering how many that are using it.

This project is very close to our hearts; everyone in the group knows someone who has died or suffered from a disease. For example, in US, 1 in 2 women and 1 in 3 men will develop cancer in their lifetime, and that is only one disease out of thousands.

Together we are strong

We were all involved in almost every part of the project. Together we have met our stake holder, created slides, brainstormed, decided on and designed our visual mappings and structures. We have tried to, by our best effort, to manage the data and transform it into something we can use in our project. Frontend is a big interest for all of us so that is also one of the things we have split between us.
